## 🔴 HIGH RISK（诈骗/FRAUD）

### 🇬🇧 English (High Risk - TNG Account Takeover)
[Caller]: Hello, this is the Touch 'n Go Security Department. We detected an unauthorized login to your eWallet from a device in Johor.
[Receiver]: What? But I am in Kampar.
[Caller]: Exactly. Someone is trying to transfer your RM800 balance to a third-party account. If you don't act now, your wallet will be permanently locked.
[Receiver]: How do I stop it?
[Caller]: I need to verify your IC number first to prove you are the owner.
[Receiver]: Is this really necessary?
[Caller]: Sir, time is running out. The system is processing the transfer. You will receive a 6-digit OTP code now. Read it to me immediately to cancel the transaction.
[Receiver]: But the SMS says don't share the code...
[Caller]: This is an emergency override. If you don't give it, your money is gone. Do it now!

### 🇲🇾 Malay (High Risk - Government Aid/STR Scam)
[Caller]: Hello, ini dari Jabatan Kebajikan Masyarakat berkaitan bantuan STR (Sumbangan Tunai Rahmah) encik.
[Receiver]: Ya, saya ada mohon. Ada masalah ke?
[Caller]: Kami dapati maklumat akaun atau nombor telefon encik tidak lengkap. Kalau tak update hari ni, bayaran RM600 akan dibatalkan.
[Receiver]: Oh, macam mana nak buat? Saya tak ada akaun bank, saya guna TNG saja.
[Caller]: Tak apa, kami boleh masukkan ke e-wallet. Tapi saya perlukan nombor IC dan kod pengesahan yang baru masuk ke telefon encik.
[Receiver]: Kod apa tu?
[Caller]: Itu kod 'System Verification'. Cepat encik, ramai lagi tengah tunggu giliran. Kalau lambat, nama encik kena 'blacklist' dari bantuan tahun depan.
[Receiver]: Kejap ya...
